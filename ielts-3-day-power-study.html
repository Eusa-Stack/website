Time Management
Spend no more than 20 minutes per reading passage. If stuck, move on and return. For writing, leave 5 minutes for reviewing.
🎯
Target Band 7+
Focus on task achievement, lexical range, and grammatical accuracy. Avoid memorised phrases — examiners detect rigidity instantly.
🧠
Active Practice
Don't just read — speak aloud, write by hand, and listen to varied English accents (British, Australian, North American) every day.
📝
Track Your Progress
After each session, note your score, identify weak areas (e.g. word formation, cohesion), and revisit those topics within 24 hours.
