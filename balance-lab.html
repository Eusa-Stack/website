📖 How to Play — Balance Equations
1Look at the equation: left side shows what reacts, right side shows what is produced.
2Type a number (1-9) in each box. This tells how many of that molecule you need.
3Goal: same number of each type of atom on both sides of the arrow.
Example: 🟢 2 H₂ + 1 O₂ → 2 H₂O
Left: 4×H + 2×O  |  Right: 4×H + 2×O ✅
4Click ✅ Check. Green = correct, red = try again!
💡 Tip: Count atoms on each side. If they match, you balanced it!
